How Can I Help You?



I'm a HR Consultant, & Python and Flutter Developer.

I'm a curious and continuously growing Professional with a passion in data analyses, storytelling, organizational behaviour, and people management.



In the past decade, I have had the opportunity to work in various industries, including academic research institutions, international technology corporations, not-for-profit as well as for-profit government organizations, in both unionized and non-unionized environments.

My exceptional ability to cultivate meaningful and trusting working relationships in each organization I've been in has enabled me to become a trusted first point of contact for many employees and leaders, especially with teammates at our satellite offices across the country.

Over the last 3 years, I continued developing my communication and leadership skills through the Toastmasters education program, where I was also given numerous chances to lead and train different leadership teams in BC. In recent months, I have immersed myself in learning how to do more interesting data analyses using the Python programming language, and how to create beautiful and functional mobile applications with Flutter, using the Dart programming language.

I would be more than happy to chat with you to see how we can work together to further leverage and enhance your teams' untapped potentials.
